ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE ?

Thirty years ago, determined with the vision of being the leaders and advocates of the women health care in the region, Dr.Ravi Bhatia and Dr.Ritu Jha started the Aarogya Hospital. Armed with more than 20 years of experience in Government sector the duo came together to offer advanced and complete Gynaecological services.

Today Aarogya Hospital is the region's leading multi-speciality hospital with the state of the art infrastructure and equipments to conduct all the Specialities and Super-specillities
services Specialists for Urology,Neurology & Neurosurgery, Psychiarty,Oncology,Cardiology,IVF/ICSI,.NICU,Joint Diseases,Emergency,Renal Diseases,General Surgery,Internal
Medicine & critical care are par excellence and positioned to offer the best care in the safest manner to every patient.

Neelam Hospital was set up with a view to provide transparent, holistic & the best medical care to the residents of North India. To achieve this goal and minimise approachability issues, we are located on the NH-64, Rajpura - Chandigarh Highway. Aarogya Hospital has a number of super speciality departments ranging from Cardiology, Gynaecology to Renal Care. Human life is precious and we are doing our best to serve every human life we as doctors, are associated with.
